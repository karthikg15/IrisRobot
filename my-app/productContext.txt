Product is a robot that monitors the patient using sensors. Camera and ultrasonic sensors to monitor the patients movements. 
Temperature sensors, IR sensor, ECG sensor, gas sensor for air quality, SpO2 sensor and heart rate monitoring. 


Patients hospitalized for extended periods often experience heightened stress, anxiety, and
isolation-challenges that are further intensified by the growing imbalance between patient volumes
and available nursing staff. Continuous monitoring and emotional reassurance become difficult to
sustain, while the burden of manual documentation and routine check-ups limits the ability of
healthcare professionals to focus on patients requiring urgent care.
IRIS is designed as an intelligent monitoring and assistance platform that bridges this gap. It continuously
observes patients' physical and behavioral indicators through connected sensors, visual inputs, and
real-time data processing, securely transmitting insights to a centralized dashboard for medical staff. By
combining automated observation with structured digital reporting, the system ensures reliable and
consistent patient oversight.
Acting as an intelligent triage layer, IRIS analyzes live data to prioritize cases based on severity, instantly
highlighting critical situations for immediate intervention. By automating monitoring, alerting, and
logging functions, it reduces administrative workload, improves response efficiency, and enables nurses
to dedicate their time and expertise to the patients who need it most-enhancing both patient safety
and hospital workflow performance.
